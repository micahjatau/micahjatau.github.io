About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage

I am a medical doctor interested in medical research, particularly public health. I graduated from the University of Jos, Nigeria, about three years ago and have worked as a medical officer in different hospitals.

Working on my undergraduate thesis helped me develop the ability to collect data, analyze it, draw conclusions about the health of a population or community, and provide solutions through health policies that lead to improved health status among the individuals of that location. My work on this project and my passion for programming and data analysis piqued my interest in medical research. So, I took up data analysis with R, where I learned about data wrangling, analysis, and visualization with tidyverse (which contains dplyr and ggplot2 ) using in-built datasets such as Palmer's penguins and mtcars. I have been working on projects with data downloaded from Kaggle and a few GitHub repositories that I intend to post. In addition, I have been learning about database management and querying using SQL. I also have skills in web development with HTML and CSS, with a basic understanding of JavaScript in website creation.

I am working on applications to graduate programs in health informatics and health data science, where I intend to study and possibly work under highly experienced teachers to learn and improve my current knowledge and skills. I possess a strong background in clinical practice, data analytics with statistical programming and biostatistics, which I believe will apply to achieving this goal.
